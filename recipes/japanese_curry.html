Japanese curry

Why Japanese curry?

You can make your own Japanese curry without leaving the house to go to a Japanese restaurant. It is authentic taste and also easy to make.

Material

Curry rice ingredients

  • 172 gr Japanese rice
  • 2 potatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 small onion
  • 450 gr beef (or chicken meat)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 237 gr water (for thickener)
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 onions (for thickener)
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 710 grams of water
  • 1 tsp chicken broth
  • 1 tsp beef broth
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp grated onion
  • 1/2 tsp masala salt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Cooking method

  1. Cut Ingredients

    2. Cut beef or chicken, potatoes, carrots and onions into small pieces.

  2. Cooking meat and vegetables

    3. Heat a skillet over medium heat, add cooking oil. Add the meat and stir-fry until evenly distributed. Enter the vegetables that have been cut, sauté again. Add 237 grams of water and bring to a boil. Lower the heat, let it cook for 40-50 minutes.

  3. Cooking curry sauce

    4. Prepare a saucepan, heat 710 grams of water and add two types of broth, meanwhile, slice two onions very thinly. Set aside. Heat butter in a new skillet, sauté the sliced onions until brown for 15-20 minutes. Add flour and curry powder, stir-fry over low heat.

  4. Prepare the japanese curry

    5. Pour the curry into the pot of meat little by little while continuing to stir. When it thickens, add the garam masala, stir, and let it simmer for 10 minutes. Sprinkle masala salt. Serve over Japanese rice.

  5. Enjoy!

    6. Eat Japanese curry by adjusting spiciness level if you like.
