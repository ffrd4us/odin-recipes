Strawberry pudding

Why strawberry pudding?

Strawberry pudding is suitable for dessert. It is light, delicious, smooth and too sweet to avoid when served at your dinner table.

Material

Layer A

  • 250gr fresh strawberries
  • 250ml of water
  • 1 pack of white jelly
  • 75gr sugar

Layer B

  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp agar-agar / konyaku powder
  • 300ml of coconut milk
  • 75gr sugar
  • 5 fresh strawberries for garnish
  • 5 tbsp white sweet condensed sauce

Cooking method

  1. Preparation

    2. Prepare five serving glasses of pudding soaked in boiled water.

  2. Cook and mix

    3. Mix all the ingredients I. Cook until boiling, pour into a serving glass until half full as a pudding base. Mix ingredients II. Cook until thickened. Pour over the strawberry pudding. Refrigerate.

  3. Serving

    4. Serve the pudding with sliced ​​fresh strawberries and a little splash of white sweet thick.
